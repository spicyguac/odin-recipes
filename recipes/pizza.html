Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is an italian dish that came about after tomatos were discovered in the Americas. It has since evolved to made out of a lot of different ingredients. This recipe is for a simple pepperoni pizza.

Although a pepperoni pizza may seem straight forward there is still lot of customization you can do. From the dough to the pepperoni you can chose

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Mozzerela Cheese
  • Pepperoni of you choice
  • Tomato Paste

Steps

  1. Make your dough
  2. Toss your dough
  3. Put your tomato paste on your dough
  4. Cover your tomato paste with a generous amount cheese
  5. Evenly distripute your desired amount of pepperoni
  6. Bake your pizza at 350 degrees for 15-20mins
