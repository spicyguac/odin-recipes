Fideo

Description

My mom likes fideo and she think my is good. I don't really know how to cook but I'm glad my mom likes my fideo. Its pretty easy to make but the key is in the little tablet of chicken flavored seasoning. Sometimes I forget to put it in and it taste really bad.

Ingredients

  • Three Medium Tomatos
  • 1/4 Of An Onion
  • A Cube Of Chicken Tomato Seasoning
  • Fideo Pasta From The Mexican Brand
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Puree The Three Medium Tomatos and the Onion with the Cube
  2. Heat up a little amount oil to slightly sautae your pasta until nice golden brown
  3. Toss your puree in the pasta fideo and stir
  4. Add two to three cups of water makes sure you don't water down the flavor
  5. Add to pinches of salt
  6. Done!!!!
