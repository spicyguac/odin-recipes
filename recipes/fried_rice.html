Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a yummy dish that is very simple to make and is great base for several other dishses. It is originally from China but the variations are very minimal. Fried rice flavor can be affected by the type of rice that is used. The type of oil can also give the fried rice more flavor or make it more healthy.

Ingredients

  • Your Choice of Rice
  • Your Choice of oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Your Choice of Mixed Veggies

Steps

  1. Cook your fried rice in a rice cooker or by pot
  2. Add a little bit of oil to you pan and toss rice in
  3. Add soy sauce to the rice
  4. Add mixed veggies to the rice
  5. Done
